Meal Name: Mustard champ
Meal ID: 53038
Area: 
Irish

Ingredients: 
1. 1kg Potatoes
2. 200ml Milk
3. 50g Butter
4. 2 tbs Mustard
5. Bunch Spring Onions
6. Bunch Spring Onions

Instructions: 
1. STEP 1Boil the potatoes for 15 mins or until tender
2.  Drain, then mash
3. STEP 2Heat the milk and half the butter in the corner of the pan, then beat into the mash, along with the wholegrain mustard
4. STEP 3Gently fry the spring onions in the remaining butter for 2 mins until just soft but still a perky green
5.  Fold into the mash and serve
6.  Great with gammon or to top a fish pie
